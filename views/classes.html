Science Kids visit us once a week and each class consists of 8 to 10 children doing the same experiments together. The class is 2-hour long and each week, Science Kids conduct two to three experiments during the 2-hour period. At the end of the experiments, children get to bring home their specimens! Feel free to chose a time slot between the hours and book a class with us.
